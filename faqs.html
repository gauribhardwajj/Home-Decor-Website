SHIPPING & DELIVERY


How long will it take for my items to arrive?

Most orders are shipped within 2 business days. Depending on your location within India, your order should reach you within 3 to 7 business days.


How do I track my order?

You will receive a SMS and email from us once your order has been dispatched with the tracking AWB number. Click on that to check your package’s current location.


Do you ship internationally?

Yes, on request. Email us at customerservice@tagspace.com with your requirement and complete shipping address and we will get back to you with a shipping quote from our courier partner. Or call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays.



RETURN & EXCHANGES


What is Tagspace's return and refund policy?

Our return process is simple and free of charge because we really want you to enjoy your Mora Taara artefacts.

  • 1. To initiate a return, email us at customerservice@tagspace.com with your order number and other details within 4 days of receiving the package. Or call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays. We will schedule the return pick-up and forward you the details.

  • 2. Hand over the goods in the original packing to the courier. It is important that you send it back in the original packing so chances of damages are minimised.

  • 3. Once we receive the item/s in good condition in our warehouse, we will initiate a refund which will reflect in your account within 7 to 10 business days. In all cases, the refund will be made via online transfer and not by cash.

How do I exchange an item?

Email us at customerservice@tagspace.com with your order number and details of the item within 4 days from the date of delivery or call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays.


I have received a damaged product. What do I do?

In case you have received a damaged or defective product let us know within 24 hours of receiving it. Include your order number and photographs of the damaged piece. Email us at customerservice@tagspace.com or call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays.


How do I cancel my order?

The order can be cancelled only if it hasn’t already been shipped. Email all details along with your order number to customerservice@morataara.com or call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays.


If I cancel my order, how will I get my refund?

After order cancellation, the money will be rolled back to your card/bank account within 7-10 working days post refund initiation. Please email us your bank account details so we can start the process.
If you are returning the goods for any reason, the amount will be transferred back to after we receive the goods back in the warehouse.

Once we receive the items in good condition in our warehouse, we will initiate the refund which will reflect in your account within 7 to 10 business
In all cases, the refund will be made via bank transfer and not by cash.

Yes, the entire GST amount will be refunded in case of cancellation/return.



INVOICES


I did not receive an invoice. What should I do?

You will receive a printed tax invoice with your package when it is delivered to you. If you would like us to email the invoice to you, please reply back on your order confirmation email or send a request to customerservice@tagspace.com. Don’t forget to mention your order number.


Can you provide a GST invoice against my online purchase?

Yes, definitely. Please email us at customerservice@tagspace.com with your order number, billing name, registered address and your GST number. Call +91-9088392120 from 10:30 am to 6:30 pm Mondays to Saturdays.

Please note the following points about GST invoices:

  1. GST invoice can be given before the 10th of the succeeding month in which your purchase was made (e.g. for a purchase made in the month of December we can issue a GST invoice only till 10th January). So please put in your request in time.

  2. If the customer lives within Haryana, the customer must have a GST number starting with 06 - otherwise even if we issue a GST invoice the customer will not be able to take credit if the GST number does not start with 06. This is as per the government GST rules under "Place of Supply".
